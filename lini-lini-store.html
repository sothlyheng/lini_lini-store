A Store Born From Passion

LiNi LiNi is a handmade K-Pop goods store created by a fan, for fans. We started on 9 September 2025 by selling Acrylic Keychain at Walk Street. — Nowaday, we have 8 members who work together to give our customers good products, good services and good prices.

Every item is made by hand with care and attention to detail. Whether it's a keychain, a sticker sheet, or a gift box — it's made with love for the K-Pop community.
